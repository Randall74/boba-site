Join Me

On my journey...

...to becoming a full-stack developer!

My plan is to learn as many aspects of the full-stack environment, in addition to the knowledge I have gained over the past 20 years in the IT field.

The plan is to literally make changes and additions to this page as I progress through the different phases of my learning cycle. I plan to keep either a blog or a to-do list that I will check off as I move along.

The ultimate goal is to create a kind of learning roadmap and pass along the information and steps I have taken, in order to make it easier for a new student - or anyone that is considering becoming a software/Web developer - to progress in their studies.
